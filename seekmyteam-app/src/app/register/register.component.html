Frequently Asked Question

How can I register?

Simple! Log in with your Purdue Address and check your Purdue Email for a confirmation to register. Happy Connecting!

Will my profile be public?

Yes! The idea is to allow yourself to be discoverable to the Boilermaker community. This will allow for users to see if you're the perfect fit and for you to find other passionate students.

Is this free?

Absolutely, save your money! SeekMyTeam understands the college budget. There are no premium or subscription based plans.

I am no longer a Purdue Student. May I still join?

Unfortunately, at this time we only support Purdue Students or Alumni with access to a purdue.edu

I am a Purdue Professor. May I still join?

Most definitely! Contact us directly to be verified and receive a badge on your profile!
